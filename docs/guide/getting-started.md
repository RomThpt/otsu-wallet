# Getting Started

This guide walks you through setting up Otsu Wallet for the first time.

## Creating a New Wallet

1. After installing the extension, click the Otsu icon in your browser toolbar
2. Click **Create a new wallet**
3. Set a strong password - this encrypts all your data locally
4. Write down your **recovery phrase** (mnemonic) and store it safely
5. Confirm your recovery phrase
6. Your wallet is ready to use

::: warning
Your recovery phrase is the only way to restore your wallet. If you lose it, your funds are unrecoverable. Never share it with anyone.
:::

## Importing an Existing Wallet

If you already have an XRPL account:

1. Click **Import an existing wallet**
2. Set a password
3. Choose your import method:
   - **Mnemonic phrase** - Enter your 12 or 24-word recovery phrase
   - **Seed** - Enter your XRPL secret seed (starts with `s`)
4. Your accounts will be restored

## Navigating the Wallet

Once set up, the main interface shows:

- **Balance** - Your total balance across all accounts
- **Accounts** - Switch between XRPL and EVM accounts
- **Tokens** - View your token balances and trustlines
- **NFTs** - Browse your NFT collection
- **Activity** - Transaction history
- **Settings** - Network selection, theme, and security options

## Switching Networks

Otsu supports multiple networks. To switch:

1. Open **Settings**
2. Select the desired network (Mainnet, Testnet, or EVM Sidechain)
3. Your balances and history will update for the selected network
